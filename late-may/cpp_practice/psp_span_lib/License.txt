psp_span_lib — Petra's Span library (static form)

A small teaching library that pairs a public header
(`include/psp_span/span.h`) with a static archive
(`lib/libpsp_span_lib.a`) holding explicit instantiations of
`psp::Span<int>`, `psp::Span<const int>`, and `psp::Span<double>`.
